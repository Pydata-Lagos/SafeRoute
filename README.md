# SafeRoute

A community platform for reporting road safety incidents across Lagos. Built by the PyData Lagos community.

## Description

Lagos commuters deal with safety threats daily: robberies, harassment, roadblocks, with no quick way to flag them or know the danger zones ahead of time. SafeRoute is a platform where anyone can report safety incidents they experience on their commute, anonymously, and see areas where such incidents have been reported.

We are trying to give people a way to surface what's happening, so communities stay informed and patterns become visible.

## What You Can Report

- Robberies
- Assault / violence
- Harassment
- Roadblocks
